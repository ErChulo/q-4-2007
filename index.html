Review of D'Moivre model of parameter $\omega$

  • By definition, \[ f(t) = \begin{cases} \frac{1}{\omega} & t \in [0,\omega)\\ 0 & t \notin [0,\omega) \end{cases} \]
  • \[ F(t) = \begin{cases} 0 & t\leq 0 \\ \frac{t}{\omega} & 0\leq t\lt \omega \\ 1 & t\ge\omega \end{cases} \]
  • $S(t)=1-F(t)=1-\frac{t}{\omega}$

  • $\mu(t)=\frac{-\frac{dS(t)}{dt}}{S(t)}=-\frac{-\frac{1}{\omega} } {1-\frac{t}{\omega}} =\frac{1}{\omega-t} $
